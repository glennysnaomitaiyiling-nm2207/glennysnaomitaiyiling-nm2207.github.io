What is the question you plan to answer?

How severe is the income inequality situation in Singapore?

Why is this an important question?

Singapore is a country that prides itself on great economic growth and good quality of life. While these things may be true, I believe there is still a significant portion of the population that falls through the cracks despite government measures, and that many peopple aren't aware of precisely because Singapore is a wealthy nation overall. I've also often heard the notion that poor people are poor simply because they haven't worked hard enough, and would like to question this idea by finding out other factors that could contribute to it.
